ABOUT ISÆBELLA

OUR HISTORY

Isæbella is a small company created by a mother of two children’s. It started as a small personal maternity dream and then a project. Since day one, all products are handmade in small batches or even one by one. We put a lot of pressure on the sustainability of our goods, starting from the supplies and ending with the final product.

Although the company has grown since the beginning, we still put a lot of pressure on the quality of our goods. We thrive on providing the best natural, organic and sustainable products for our customers to enjoy. One product at a time.
